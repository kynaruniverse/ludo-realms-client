How to Play
🎲
Roll to Move
Tap ROLL each turn. Tap a glowing square to move your token there.
🚪
Exit Your Yard
Roll a 6 to bring a token out of your yard onto the board. Rolling a 6 gives you an extra turn.
💥
Captures
Land on an opponent's token to send it back to their yard. Stars are safe squares — no captures there.
🛡️
Blocks
Two of your tokens on the same square form a block. Opponents cannot pass through or capture a block.
🏠
Getting Home
Guide all 4 tokens along the coloured home column into the centre. You need an exact roll to enter each home square.
⚡
Power-Ups
Land on a ⚡ square to collect a power-up. Use it on your turn for special effects like guaranteed 6, swapping tokens, or cursing opponents.
✦
Mana
Power-ups cost mana. You start with 3, gain 1 per turn, up to a max of 5. Shown in the top-right during a game.
🏆
Win Condition
First player to move all 4 tokens to the centre finish wins!
