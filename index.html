How to Play
🎲
Roll to move
Tap ROLL. Tap a glowing square to move your token there.
🚪
Exit your yard
Roll a 6 to bring a token out. Your yard token will glow — tap it to move it out.
💥
Captures
Land on an opponent to send them back. Stars ⭐ are safe — no captures.
🛡️
Blocks
Two of your tokens on one square = a block. Opponents can't pass through.
6️⃣
Extra turn
Roll a 6 to get another roll. Three 6s in a row = forfeit.
🏠
Get home
Guide all 4 tokens along your coloured column to the centre. Exact roll needed.
⚡
Power-ups
Land on ⚡ to collect. Guaranteed 6, Swap, or Curse — spend mana to activate.
🏆
Win
First player to get all 4 tokens home wins!
